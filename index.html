Rwanda

Rwanda is a small but beautiful country in East Africa known as the "Land of a Thousand Hills." It is famous for its breathtaking mountains, rich culture, and wildlife such as the mountain gorillas found in Volcanoes National Park. Rwanda is also known for being one of the cleanest and safest countries in Africa. Kigali, the capital city, is known for its cleanliness and modern development.
